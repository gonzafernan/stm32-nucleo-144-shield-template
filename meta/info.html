Template for designing shields compatible with STM32 Nucleo-144 development boards (NUCLEO-H743ZI, NUCLEO-F429ZI, NUCLEO-F746ZG and similar).

Includes the board outline, four shield connectors pre-placed (CN8, CN9, CN7, CN10/Zio), all STM32 GPIO ports exposed as named nets, and power rails (+3V3, +5V, GND, AVDD, IOREF, VIN).
